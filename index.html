About Me

The goal of this website is to help prevent you from going off to college and finding yourself alone and without a sense of direction.

"Attending a university is pivotal point in life to many who seek a brighter future. Unfortunately, for many first-generation students this shift in their life can be a lot to bear. It is for this reason that I hope my experiences will be able provide insight into a life as a first-generation college student, thus eliminating one's bare perception."
